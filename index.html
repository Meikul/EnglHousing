Meal Plans

  • Required for students who don't have access to kitchens.
  • Increasingly, dorms are being built without access to a full kitchen.
  • Pay $10 a meal for passable food.
  • Limited selection for those with dietary restrictions.

Parking

  • There is NO free parking for on-campus housing at USU.
  • There are only 8,924 parking spots available to students and staff.
  • More than 3500 students live on campus.

Cramped Quarters

  • Shared rooms have the same dimensions as private rooms but are shared between two people.
  • Many on-campus dorms can have up to eight people with as little as two bathrooms, leading to conflict with roommates.
  • On-campus housing at USU has a difference of $500-$700 per semester between shared and private rooms.
  • LLC shared bedrooms are 12’ x 16’ whereas private bedrooms are 11’ x 17’, with a difference of about $600.

Make a Change

  • No one should be forced into a meal plan. All housing should have kitchen access.
  • USU should stop with the shiny new buildings and work on what would actually impact students, affordable housing.
  • USU needs to protect students' access to campus. Old housing being removed should be replaced with new housing.
